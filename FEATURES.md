# Disko Gym Web Page Features

Disko Gym is a single-page gym landing website built with React and Vite. The page is designed to present the gym brand, facilities, pricing, trainers, and contact details in a visually strong, mobile-friendly format.

## Core Features

- Sticky navigation with anchor links to page sections.
- Full-screen hero section with primary calls to action.
- About section with gym highlights and feature cards.
- Facilities section showcasing training zones and services.
- Membership pricing section with three plan options.
- Responsive gallery section using a masonry layout.
- Trainers section with profile cards and hover quotes.
- Contact section with embedded Google Map and business info.
- Final call-to-action banner encouraging sign-ups.
- Footer with contact details, opening hours, and social icons.

## UX and Visual Features

- Scroll-triggered reveal animations across major sections.
- Desktop and mobile navigation, including a collapsible mobile menu.
- Strong visual branding with neon accents, glass-style cards, and bold typography.
- Responsive layouts for mobile, tablet, and desktop breakpoints.
- Hover effects on cards, gallery items, facility tiles, and trainer profiles.

## Current Content Included

- Gym location in Sarajevo.
- Opening hours listed as daily from 09:00 to 24:00.
- Contact phone number and email address.
- Membership plans: Classic, VIP, and Student.
- Highlighted services such as strength training, cardio, group fitness, aerobic studio, personal training, and recovery area.

## Notes

- The current buttons are presentation-focused and do not yet connect to booking, registration, or payment flows.
- Social links currently use placeholder `#` URLs.
- Images are loaded from external sources.
